Dada (Former Indian Cricketer and Captain)

Sourav Chandidas Ganguly, born 8 July 1972 , affectionately known as Dada (meaning "elder brother" in Bengali), is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team, Currently, he is appointed as the President of the Cricket Association of Bengal and President of the Editorial Board with Wisden India.
Ganguly established himself as one of the world's leading batsmen and also one of the greatest captains of the national cricket team.While batting, he was especially prolific through the off side, earning himself the nickname God of the Off Side for his elegant strokeplay square of the wicket and through the covers.

Sachin Tendulkar

"God of Cricket"

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar; born 24 April 1973, is a former Indian international cricketer and a former captain of the Indian national team. He is the highest run scorer of all time in International cricket. Tendulkar took up cricket at the age of eleven, made his Test debut on 15 November 1989 against Pakistan in Karachi at the age of sixteen. He is the only player to have scored one hundred international centuries, the first batsman to score a double century in a ODI.
